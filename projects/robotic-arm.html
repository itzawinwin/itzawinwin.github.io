Junior Design Fall 2024 - Spring 2025 COMPLETED

Project Overview

Collaborated with a team of six to design, develop, and build a 4-axis robotic arm from the ground up, including conceptual design, constraint calculations, kinematics analysis, and final construction.

Project Role: Control Engineer ‚Äì Designed control circuits and Arduino programs for manual and autonomous pick-and-place operations with optimized runtime.

Specifications

  • Team Size: 4 students
  • Duration: 3 months
  • Budget: $200
  • Reach: 20 cm radius
  • Accuracy: ¬±2 mm positioning

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Precision Control

Problem: Gear slippage and loss of precision in arm movement

Solution: Implement direct drive system and change out servo with higher precision encoder for better accuracy

Challenge: Object Detection

Problem: Inconsistent detection of metal balls due to reflective surfaces of the base plate

Solution: Train better scan position and implement search first pick later approach
